About Us

At Helping Hand BD, we aim to rebuild hope where it’s been damaged or lost. We work with those in need, those who are underprivileged, and those who are underserved to rebuild and restructure.

Our focus lies on community efforts, shelter, food aid, clean water, community awareness, and educational opportunities.It is also HHBD's hope to raise awareness in the rural areas of extending disaster relief to the less fortunate and creating a network of organizations linking the various relief efforts.
